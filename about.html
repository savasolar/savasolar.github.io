About

I’m Sava, a software designer, coder, and musician based in Toronto, Canada.


I got into coding and design when I was 10 years old. That’s when I found out you could type stuff into a text editor, save it as an HTML file, and have it open as an interactive page in your browser. Instantly, I was hooked.


On this journey, I learned (and am still learning) to use whatever tools necessary in order to make my ideas come to life—including photo/video editing, web design, games, and anything else I find fun!


Most recently, I’ve been merging my love for software design with my love for making music, creating audio plugins that open a world of possibilities in the audio production process.


For me, coding and design never stopped being about having fun and bringing cool ideas to life with friends.
